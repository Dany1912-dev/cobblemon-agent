## WHAT ARE MEGA EVOLUTIONS?
- Temporary evolution during battle using a Mega Stone
- Only one Mega Evolution per battle (per team)
- Pokemon reverts after battle ends
- Mega Evolution gives: stat boost (+100 BST total), possible type change, possible ability change
- Requires: matching Mega Stone held by Pokemon + Mega Ring/Key Stone on trainer
- In Cobblemon: likely activated via Mega Stone item + key item

### SPECIAL CASES
- **Mega Rayquaza**: Does NOT need a Mega Stone! Only needs to know the move Dragon Ascent. Does NOT use the Mega Ring slot (can hold another item).
- **Primal Reversion** (similar to Mega): Primal Groudon (Red Orb, Fire/Ground, Desolate Land) and Primal Kyogre (Blue Orb, Water, Primordial Sea). +100 BST each.

## MEGA ABILITIES EXPLAINED
- **Tough Claws**: +30% damage to contact moves
- **Drought / Drizzle / Sand Stream / Snow Warning**: Sets weather on entry
- **Mega Launcher**: +50% power to aura/pulse moves (Dark Pulse, Water Pulse, Heal Pulse, Aura Sphere, Dragon Pulse)
- **Parental Bond**: Attacks hit twice (second at 25% power)
- **Aerilate / Pixilate / Refrigerate**: Normal moves become Flying/Fairy/Ice and get +20% power
- **Shadow Tag**: Opponent cannot switch
- **Mold Breaker**: Ignores opponent abilities
- **Technician**: +50% power to moves with ≤60 base power
- **Skill Link**: Multi-hit moves always hit 5 times
- **Huge Power / Pure Power**: Doubles Attack stat
- **Magic Bounce**: Reflects status moves
- **Adaptability**: STAB becomes 2x instead of 1.5x
- **Speed Boost**: +1 Speed each turn
- **Intimidate**: -1 Attack on opponent at entry
- **Prankster**: +1 priority to status moves
- **Strong Jaw**: +50% to bite moves
- **Sheer Force**: Removes secondary effects for +30% power
- **No Guard**: All moves hit (both sides)
- **Filter**: Reduces super-effective damage by 25%
- **Scrappy**: Can hit Ghost types with Normal/Fighting moves
- **Delta Stream**: Negates Flying-type weaknesses, cancels other weather
- **Desolate Land / Primordial Sea**: Extreme weather, Water/Fire moves fail
- **Sand Force**: +30% Rock/Ground/Steel moves in Sandstorm
- **Swift Swim**: 2x Speed in Rain
- **Lightning Rod**: Draws Electric moves, +1 SpA when hit
- **Solar Power**: +50% SpA in Sun, loses 1/8 HP per turn
- **Thick Fat**: Halves Fire and Ice damage
- **Insomnia**: Cannot fall asleep
- **Steadfast**: +1 Speed when flinched
- **Trace**: Copies opponent's ability
- **Levitate**: Ground immunity
- **Healer**: 30% chance to heal ally's status each turn
- **Inner Focus**: Cannot flinch, blocks Intimidate

NOTE: Mega Evolutions in Cobblemon may function depending on the mod version and complementary mods installed. The mechanics follow standard Pokémon rules.
