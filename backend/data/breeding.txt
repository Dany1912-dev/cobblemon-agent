## BREEDING BASICS
- Two compatible Pokemon left together can produce an Egg
- The Egg hatches into a level 1 Pokemon of the female's species (or non-Ditto parent)
- Eggs are produced at a rate based on the parents' relationship
- "They prefer to play with other Pokemon" = same species, different OT = fastest
- "They get along" = same species same OT, or different species = normal
- "They don't seem to like each other" = different species, different OT = slow
- "They prefer to play with other Pokemon" (not each other) = no eggs

## GENDER RATIOS
- 50% Male / 50% Female: Most Pokemon
- 87.5% Male / 12.5% Female: Starters, Eevee, Snorlax, fossils, etc.
- 25% Male / 75% Female: Some species (Clefairy, Jigglypuff, etc.)
- 100% Male / 0% Female: Hitmons, Tauros, Braviary, Throh, Sawk, etc.
- 0% Male / 100% Female: Chansey, Jynx, Kangaskhan, Miltank, Petilil, Vullaby, Flabébé, etc.
- Gender Unknown: Magnemite, Voltorb, Staryu, Porygon, Shedinja, Lunatone, Solrock, Beldum, etc. (can only breed with Ditto)

## EGG MOVES
- A baby Pokemon can inherit certain moves from its parents
- If both parents know a move the baby can learn by level-up, the baby will have it
- If the father knows a TM/TR/egg move compatible with the baby, it's inherited
- In Cobblemon, egg moves may be obtainable via the Move Tutor or from wild spawns

## INCENSE BREEDING
Some Pokemon require a parent to hold a specific incense to produce baby forms:
- Sea Incense: Marill/Azumarill → Azurill
- Lax Incense: Wobbuffet → Wynaut
- Rose Incense: Roselia/Roserade → Budew
- Pure Incense: Chimecho → Chingling
- Luck Incense: Chansey/Blissey → Happiny
- Full Incense: Snorlax → Munchlax
- Wave Incense: Mantine → Mantyke
- Rock Incense: Sudowoodo → Bonsly

## HATCHING
- Eggs hatch after a certain number of steps (egg cycles)
- Each Pokemon species has a different hatch time (e.g., Magikarp = fast, Dratini = slow)
- Flame Body / Magma Armor ability halves egg hatch time
- In Cobblemon, eggs likely hatch based on ticks/play time

## BREEDING FOR IVs
- Destiny Knot: if one parent holds it, 5 IVs are passed down instead of 3
- Power Items: guarantee passing down a specific stat's IV (Power Weight = HP, Power Bracer = Atk, etc.)
- Everstone: passes down the parent's Nature 100% of the time

NOTE: Breeding mechanics in Cobblemon may depend on which complementary mods are installed. Standard Pokémon breeding rules are the default assumption.
