# ============================================================
# POKEMON STATS & TRAINING REFERENCE
# EVs, IVs, Natures, Base Stats
# ============================================================

## SIX STATS
- HP: Hit Points - determines how much damage a Pokemon can take
- Attack: Physical damage output
- Defense: Physical damage resistance
- Special Attack: Special damage output
- Special Defense: Special damage resistance
- Speed: Determines move order in battle (higher goes first, unless priority moves)

## BASE STATS
Each Pokemon species has fixed base stats. Combined with IVs, EVs, and Nature, these determine the actual stats.

### Base Stat Total (BST) Tiers
- 720: Arceus
- 680: Legendary cover Pokemon (Mewtwo, Rayquaza, Dialga, etc.)
- 600: Pseudo-legendaries (Dragonite, Tyranitar, Salamence, Metagross, Garchomp, Hydreigon, Goodra, Kommo-o, Dragapult, Baxcalibur)
- 570-580: Legendary trios, Ultra Beasts
- 525-540: Fully evolved starters
- 450-500: Most fully evolved "standard" Pokemon
- 300-400: Middle evolutions and strong single-stage Pokemon
- 175-250: Baby Pokemon and early bugs

## IVs (INDIVIDUAL VALUES)
- Range: 0-31 for each stat
- Determined when the Pokemon spawns or hatches
- Cannot be changed (except through Hyper Training to max at 31)
- At level 100, each IV equals 1 stat point
- "Perfect" or "6IV" means 31 in all 6 stats
- For Hidden Power (if in Cobblemon), IV spread determines type
- For Trick Room teams, 0 Speed IV is ideal

## EVs (EFFORT VALUES)
- Range: 0-252 per stat, 510 total max across all stats
- Gained by defeating Pokemon or using vitamins (HP Up, Protein, Iron, etc.)
- At level 100: 4 EVs = 1 stat point (so 252 EVs = +63 to that stat)
- Common EV spreads:
  - 252 Atk / 252 Spe / 4 HP: Fast physical sweeper
  - 252 SpA / 252 Spe / 4 HP: Fast special sweeper
  - 252 HP / 252 Atk / 4 Def: Bulky physical attacker
  - 252 HP / 252 Def / 4 SpD: Physical wall
  - 252 HP / 252 SpD / 4 Def: Special wall

### EV Training Items
- Power Weight: +8 HP EVs per KO
- Power Bracer: +8 Attack EVs per KO
- Power Belt: +8 Defense EVs per KO
- Power Lens: +8 Sp. Atk EVs per KO
- Power Band: +8 Sp. Def EVs per KO
- Power Anklet: +8 Speed EVs per KO

### EV-Reducing Berries
- Pomeg Berry: -10 HP EVs
- Kelpsy Berry: -10 Attack EVs
- Qualot Berry: -10 Defense EVs
- Hondew Berry: -10 Sp. Atk EVs
- Grepa Berry: -10 Sp. Def EVs
- Tamato Berry: -10 Speed EVs

### Vitamins (add 10 EVs each, up to 100 per stat)
- HP Up, Protein, Iron, Calcium, Zinc, Carbos

## NATURES (25 total, 5 neutral + 20 stat-modifying)
Each Nature raises one stat by 10% and lowers another by 10% (except neutral natures).

### Nature Mints (Cobblemon: Pink Mint Leaf + crafting)
Mints change a Pokemon's stat growth to match a specific nature without changing the actual nature.

## STAT CALCULATION
At level 50 (common competitive level):
- HP = floor((2×Base + IV + floor(EV/4)) × Level/100) + Level + 10
- Other Stats = floor((2×Base + IV + floor(EV/4)) × Level/100 + 5) × Nature

At level 100:
- Each IV = 1 stat point
- 4 EVs = 1 stat point
- Nature = 10% boost/reduction

NOTE: Cobblemon likely scales stats to match Minecraft's real-time combat if it doesn't use turn-based battles. The IV/EV/Nature system is still relevant for competitive play and breeding.
