## NATURAL BERRIES (30)

### Status-Curing Berries
- Cheri Berry: Cures paralysis when held. Pokemon consumes it automatically when paralyzed.
- Chesto Berry: Cures sleep when held. Pokemon consumes it automatically when asleep.
- Pecha Berry: Cures poison when held. Pokemon consumes it automatically when poisoned.
- Rawst Berry: Cures burn when held. Pokemon consumes it automatically when burned.
- Aspear Berry: Cures freeze when held. Pokemon consumes it automatically when frozen.
- Persim Berry: Cures confusion when held. Pokemon consumes it automatically when confused.

### HP Restoration
- Oran Berry: Restores 10 HP when holder's HP drops to 1/2 max or less.
- Sitrus Berry: Restores 1/4 max HP when holder's HP drops to 1/2 max or less.

### Type-Resist Berries (halve damage from one super effective attack of the listed type)
- Occa Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Fire-type attack.
- Passho Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Water-type attack.
- Wacan Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Electric-type attack.
- Rindo Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Grass-type attack.
- Yache Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Ice-type attack.
- Chople Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Fighting-type attack.
- Kebia Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Poison-type attack.
- Shuca Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Ground-type attack.
- Coba Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Flying-type attack.
- Payapa Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Psychic-type attack.
- Tanga Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Bug-type attack.
- Charti Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Rock-type attack.
- Kasib Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Ghost-type attack.
- Haban Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Dragon-type attack.
- Colbur Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Dark-type attack.
- Babiri Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Steel-type attack.
- Chilan Berry: Halves damage from one Normal-type attack.
- Roseli Berry: Halves damage from one super effective Fairy-type attack.

### Flavor Berries (used for bait, seasoning, and cooking)
- Razz Berry: Used as bait ingredient and for crafting. No battle effect.
- Bluk Berry: Used as bait ingredient and for crafting. No battle effect.
- Nanab Berry: Used as bait ingredient and for crafting. No battle effect.
- Wepear Berry: Used as bait ingredient and for crafting. No battle effect.
- Pinap Berry: Used as bait ingredient and for crafting. No battle effect.

## MUTATED BERRIES (40)

### PP Restoration
- Leppa Berry: Restores 10 PP to the first move that reaches 0 PP. Works in and out of battle.
- Hopo Berry: Cannot be eaten by holder. Restores 10 PP to any move when used from inventory.

### Full Status Cure
- Lum Berry: Cures any non-volatile status condition (burn, poison, paralysis, sleep, freeze) and confusion.
- Touga Berry: Cures confusion only.

### HP Restoration (Pinch Berries - activate at 1/4 HP or less)
- Figy Berry: Restores 1/3 max HP at 1/4 HP or less. Confuses Pokemon with Attack-lowering natures (Lonely, Adamant, Naughty, Brave).
- Iapapa Berry: Restores 1/3 max HP at 1/4 HP or less. Confuses Pokemon with Defense-lowering natures (Bold, Relaxed, Impish, Lax).
- Wiki Berry: Restores 1/3 max HP at 1/4 HP or less. Confuses Pokemon with Special Attack-lowering natures (Modest, Mild, Quiet, Rash).
- Aguav Berry: Restores 1/3 max HP at 1/4 HP or less. Confuses Pokemon with Special Defense-lowering natures (Calm, Gentle, Sassy, Careful).
- Mago Berry: Restores 1/3 max HP at 1/4 HP or less. Confuses Pokemon with Speed-lowering natures (Timid, Hasty, Jolly, Naive).

### Super-Effective Hit Triggers
- Enigma Berry: Restores 1/4 max HP after being hit by a super effective move.
- Jaboca Berry: If hit by a physical move, the attacker loses 1/8 of its max HP.
- Rowap Berry: If hit by a special move, the attacker loses 1/8 of its max HP.

### Stat-Boosting Pinch Berries (activate at 1/4 HP or less)
- Liechi Berry: Raises Attack by 1 stage.
- Ganlon Berry: Raises Defense by 1 stage.
- Salac Berry: Raises Speed by 1 stage.
- Petaya Berry: Raises Special Attack by 1 stage.
- Apicot Berry: Raises Special Defense by 1 stage.
- Starf Berry: Raises a random stat by 2 stages.
- Lansat Berry: Raises Critical Hit ratio by 1 stage.
- Micle Berry: Holder gains 1.2x accuracy for its next move.
- Custap Berry: Holder moves first in its priority bracket on the next turn.

### Defensive Stat Boost on Hit
- Kee Berry: Raises Defense by 1 stage after being hit by a physical attack.
- Maranga Berry: Raises Special Defense by 1 stage after being hit by a special attack.

### EV-Reducing Berries (lowers EVs by 10, raises friendship)
- Pomeg Berry: Lowers HP EVs by 10.
- Kelpsy Berry: Lowers Attack EVs by 10.
- Qualot Berry: Lowers Defense EVs by 10.
- Hondew Berry: Lowers Special Attack EVs by 10.
- Grepa Berry: Lowers Special Defense EVs by 10.
- Tamato Berry: Lowers Speed EVs by 10.

### Other
- Eggant Berry: Cures infatuation when held.
- Cornn Berry: Used for crafting and cooking. No battle effect.
- Magost Berry: Used for crafting and cooking. No battle effect.
- Rabuta Berry: Used for crafting and cooking. No battle effect.
- Nomel Berry: Used for crafting and cooking. No battle effect.
- Spelon Berry: Used for crafting and cooking. No battle effect.
- Pamtre Berry: Used for crafting and cooking. No battle effect.
- Watmel Berry: Used for crafting and cooking. No battle effect.
- Durin Berry: Used for crafting and cooking. No battle effect.
- Belue Berry: Used for crafting and cooking. No battle effect.

## BERRY USES
- Held Item: Most berries can be held by Pokemon for automatic battle effects.
- Bait: Berries can be used as bait on a Poke Rod for fishing.
- Seasoning: Berries serve as flavor ingredients in recipes.
- Crafting: Berries + Bowl = Berry Juice. Used in brewing Potions, Vitamins, Ethers, status heals.
- Cooking: Used to make Candies, Mochi, Poke Cake, and other food items.
- Farming: Berries can be planted and grown on farmland. Mutated berries come from cross-pollination.
