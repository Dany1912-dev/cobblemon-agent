## SPAWNING MECHANICS
- Pokemon spawn naturally in specific biomes depending on their species
- Spawns can be affected by: time of day, weather, nearby blocks, Y-level, structures
- Some Pokemon only spawn in specific structures (villages, temples, etc.)
- Legendary Pokemon have special spawn conditions (often via shrines or structures)
- Water Pokemon spawn in oceans, rivers, lakes, swamps
- Underground Pokemon spawn in caves and at lower Y-levels
- Some Pokemon require specific blocks nearby (flowers, stone types, etc.)
