## WHAT ARE ABILITIES?
Each Pokemon has one ability (sometimes two or three possible). Abilities provide passive effects in battle or in the overworld.

## NOTABLE OVERWORLD EFFECTS (useful in Cobblemon)
- Flame Body / Magma Armor: Halves egg hatch time
- Illuminate / Arena Trap / No Guard: Increases wild Pokemon encounter rate
- Stench / White Smoke / Quick Feet: Decreases wild Pokemon encounter rate
- Pickup: Pokemon may pick up items after battles
- Compound Eyes: Increases chance of held items on wild Pokemon
- Cute Charm: 66% chance wild Pokemon is opposite gender
- Synchronize: 50% chance wild Pokemon has same nature
- Magnet Pull: More Steel-type encounters
- Static: More Electric-type encounters
- Storm Drain: More Water-type encounters
- Intimidate / Pressure: Prevents lower-level wild Pokemon from spawning
- Suction Cups / Sticky Hold: Increases bite rate when fishing
- Honey Gather: Pokemon may gather Honey after battle
- Run Away / Smoke Ball: Guarantees escape from wild Pokemon

## TOP TIER BATTLE ABILITIES
- Intimidate: Lowers opponent's Attack by 1 stage on entry
- Levitate: Immune to Ground-type moves (and Spikes/Toxic Spikes)
- Prankster: Non-damaging moves have +1 priority
- Magic Guard: Only takes direct damage (no status, weather, entry hazards, Life Orb)
- Magic Bounce: Reflects status moves back at the user
- Speed Boost: Raises Speed by 1 stage each turn
- Regenerator: Restores 1/3 max HP when switching out
- Multiscale / Shadow Shield: Takes half damage at full HP
- Beast Boost: Raises highest stat on KO
- Protean / Libero: Changes type to match the move being used
- Adaptability: STAB bonus becomes 2x instead of 1.5x
- Huge Power / Pure Power: Doubles Attack stat
- Technician: Moves with ≤60 base power deal 1.5x damage
- Mold Breaker / Teravolt / Turboblaze: Ignores opponent's abilities
- Sturdy: Cannot be KO'd in one hit from full HP
- Iron Barbs / Rough Skin: Contact moves deal 1/8 HP recoil
- Serene Grace: Doubles secondary effect chance
- Sheer Force: Removes secondary effects for 1.3x power
- Contrary: Reverses stat changes (boosts become drops and vice versa)
- Guts: Attack boosted 1.5x when statused (ignores burn Attack drop)
- Sand Stream / Drizzle / Drought / Snow Warning: Sets weather on entry
- Electric / Psychic / Grassy / Misty Surge: Sets terrain on entry
- Unaware: Ignores opponent's stat changes
- Shadow Tag / Arena Trap: Prevents opponent from switching
- Wonder Guard: Only hit by super effective moves (Shedinja exclusive)

## HIDDEN ABILITIES
Most Pokemon have a rare "Hidden Ability" that must be obtained through special means:
- Patches in Cobblemon (Ability Patch item)
- Special wild encounters
- Raids / bosses

## ABILITY TYPES
1. Standard Ability (slot 1 or 2): Normal abilities the Pokemon can have
2. Hidden Ability: Rare ability only obtainable through special means
3. Some species have only one possible ability (e.g., Levitate on Rotom)

NOTE: Cobblemon implements most standard Pokémon abilities. Some abilities that rely on turn-based mechanics may function slightly differently in the real-time Minecraft environment.
