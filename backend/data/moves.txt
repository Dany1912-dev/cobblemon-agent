## MOVE CATEGORIES
- Physical: Uses Attack stat and is affected by burn, Intimidate, etc. Contact moves trigger abilities like Static, Flame Body.
- Special: Uses Special Attack stat. Not affected by burn's Attack drop.
- Status: Non-damaging moves that buff, debuff, inflict status, set weather/terrain, etc.

## MOVE PRIORITY
Some moves have increased or decreased priority:
- +5: Helping Hand
- +4: Baneful Bunker, Detect, Endure, King's Shield, Magic Coat, Obstruct, Protect, Silk Trap, Snatch, Spiky Shield
- +3: Fake Out, Quick Guard, Wide Guard
- +2: Ally Switch, Extreme Speed, Feint, First Impression, Follow Me, Rage Powder
- +1: Accelerock, Aqua Jet, Baby-Doll Eyes, Bullet Punch, Grassy Glide, Ice Shard, Mach Punch, Prankster moves, Quick Attack, Shadow Sneak, Sucker Punch, Vacuum Wave, Water Shuriken
- 0: Most moves
- -1: Vital Throw
- -3: Beak Blast, Focus Punch, Shell Trap
- -5: Avalanche, Revenge
- -6: Circle Throw, Dragon Tail, Roar, Whirlwind
- -7: Trick Room, Magic Room, Wonder Room

## IMPORTANT STATUS MOVES
### Entry Hazards
- Stealth Rock: Deals Rock-type damage based on type effectiveness (1/8 to 1/2 HP). Most important hazard.
- Spikes: Up to 3 layers (1/8, 1/6, 1/4 HP). Grounded Pokemon only.
- Toxic Spikes: Up to 2 layers (poison, bad poison). Grounded, non-Steel, non-Poison types.
- Sticky Web: Lowers Speed by 1 stage. Grounded Pokemon only.

### Hazard Removal
- Rapid Spin: Removes hazards on user's side, raises Speed. 50 BP Normal physical.
- Defog: Removes hazards on BOTH sides, lowers opponent's evasion.
- Mortal Spin: Removes hazards, poisons opponent. 30 BP Poison physical.

### Setup Moves
- Swords Dance: +2 Attack
- Nasty Plot: +2 Special Attack
- Dragon Dance: +1 Attack, +1 Speed
- Quiver Dance: +1 Special Attack, +1 Special Defense, +1 Speed
- Calm Mind: +1 Special Attack, +1 Special Defense
- Bulk Up: +1 Attack, +1 Defense
- Shell Smash: +2 Attack, +2 Special Attack, +2 Speed, -1 Defense, -1 Special Defense
- Iron Defense: +2 Defense
- Amnesia: +2 Special Defense
- Agility / Rock Polish / Autotomize: +2 Speed
- Belly Drum: Maxes Attack (+6) but loses 50% HP

## COBBLEMON-SPECIFIC MOVE NOTES
- Moves function identically to main Pokémon games
- PP system works the same (5-40 PP per move)
- STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus): 1.5x damage if move type matches user's type
- Physical/Special split is based on Gen 4+ classification
- Move tutors may be available in specific structures or NPCs

## TRICK ROOM (Espacio Raro / Distorsión en español)
- Move: Trick Room | Type: Psychic | Category: Status | PP: 5 (max 8) | Priority: -7
- Effect: For 5 turns, Speed order REVERSES. Slowest move first, fastest move last.
- Cancels: Using Trick Room again cancels it.
- Priority still works normally (+1, +2, Sucker Punch, etc., still go before normal moves)
- Good abusers (slow + high Attack/SpA): Ursaluna, Conkeldurr, Rhyperior, Torkoal, Iron Hands, Marowak-Alola
- Good setters: Hatterene, Cresselia, Porygon2, Slowking, Farigiraf, Indeedee-F
- Items: Room Service (consumed on entry, -1 Speed in Trick Room), Mental Herb (blocks Taunt)
- Counter: Taunt the setter, priority moves, stall with Protect, use your own Trick Room to cancel
