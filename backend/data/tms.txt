## TM FORMATS BY GENERATION
- Gen I-V: Single-use TMs
- Gen V onward: Infinite-use TMs
- Gen VIII (SwSh): 100 TMs + 100 TRs (TRs are single-use)
- Gen VIII (BDSP): 100 TMs
- Gen IX (SV): 229 TMs (infinite use)
- Legends Z-A: 107 base + 53 Mega Dimension TMs = 160 total
- Cobblemon: Uses Gen IX style with craftable/renewable TMs
